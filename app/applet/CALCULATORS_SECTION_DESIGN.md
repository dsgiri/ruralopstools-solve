# Calculators Section Design & UX Copy

## 1. Section Heading & Intro
- **Section Heading:** Estimate the damage. Plan the fix.
- **Section Intro Text:** Use our field calculators to see exactly how much a problem is costing you and what it takes to solve it. Don't guess—run the numbers.

## 2. Calculator Cards (6-10 Cards)

### 1. Water Leak Cost Calculator
- **Short Description:** Find out exactly how much a slow leak is costing your operation in wasted water and electricity.
- **Main Metric/Output:** Annual Water Loss ($ and Gallons)
- **CTA Text:** Calculate Water Loss →
- **Parent Category:** Water & Leaks

### 2. Manual Check Labor Burden
- **Short Description:** Calculate the true cost of driving out to manually check tanks, gates, and fences every week.
- **Main Metric/Output:** Wasted Drive Time & Fuel Cost
- **CTA Text:** Estimate Labor Burden →
- **Parent Category:** Remote Checks

### 3. Pump Burnout Risk Logger
- **Short Description:** Track cycle times and symptoms to gauge failure risk before the motor burns out entirely.
- **Main Metric/Output:** Failure Risk Status (Low/Med/High) & Est. Replacement Cost
- **CTA Text:** Log Pump Trouble →
- **Parent Category:** Pumps & Power

### 4. Fence Fault Cost Tracker
- **Short Description:** Estimate the true cost of recurring perimeter escapes, livestock recovery, and temporary fixes.
- **Main Metric/Output:** Annual Fault & Escape Cost
- **CTA Text:** Track Fence Faults →
- **Parent Category:** Fence Faults

### 5. Fix vs. Ignore Calculator

- **Main Metric/Output:** 5-Year Cost Comparison

- **Parent Category:** General Maintenance

### 6. Freeze Vulnerability Scorer
- **Short Description:** Assess the financial risk of exposed valves and pipes before a severe weather event strikes.
- **Main Metric/Output:** Freeze Vulnerability Score & Est. Damage Risk
- **CTA Text:** Plan Freeze Defense →
- **Parent Category:** Freeze Prep

### 7. Remote Monitor Payback Estimator
- **Short Description:** Find out exactly how long it takes for a snap-on sensor to pay for itself in saved labor and fuel.
- **Main Metric/Output:** Estimated Payback Period (Months)
- **CTA Text:** Calculate Payback Time →
- **Parent Category:** Hardware Budgeting

### 8. System Upkeep Planner
- **Short Description:** Anticipate long-term maintenance, battery replacements, and repair costs for field tech.
- **Main Metric/Output:** Annual Maintenance Run Rate
- **CTA Text:** Plan Upkeep Budget →
- **Parent Category:** Hardware Budgeting

## 5. Notes for Results-Panel Language
- **Be Direct and Absolute:** The result shouldn't say "You might save some money." It should say "You are losing $4,200 a year."
- **Visual Presentation:** The final number should be massive, bold, and use a heavy font (e.g., `Zilla Slab`). It should look like a stamp on a clipboard.
- **The "So What?":** Immediately below the final number, provide a 1-sentence reality check. (e.g., *"That's the cost of a new 5HP pump, wasted on a leaky valve."*)
- **Bridge to Hardware:** The primary action after seeing the result must be the hardware fix. (e.g., *"Stop the leak with a FlowWatch kit →"*)

---

## 6. Notes for Assumptions, Disclaimers, and Estimate-Only Messaging
- **Transparency:** If the calculator assumes $3.00/gallon for off-road diesel, state it clearly in a small, monospaced "Assumptions" box below the inputs, and let the user overwrite it.
- **The "Hazard" Box:** All calculators must include the standard legal disclaimer inside a heavily bordered, yellow-tinted box at the bottom of the page.
- **Tone of Disclaimer:** Keep it serious but plainspoken. *"Disclaimer: These figures are estimates based on standard assumptions and your inputs. This tool is for informational purposes only. We disclaim all liability for decisions, costs, or damages arising from reliance on these results. Know your own operation."*
