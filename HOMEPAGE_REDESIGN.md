## 5. Notes on Mobile Hierarchy
- **Touch Targets:** Make category pickers large, tactile buttons (min 44px height) that feel like physical switches.
- **Vertical Flow:** Stack the Problem Picker vertically on small screens, allowing operators to quickly tap their immediate issue.
- **Card Design:** Use high-contrast, border-heavy panel cards (e.g., `border-4 border-stone-800`). Hide secondary descriptive text on mobile if it clutters the screen, keeping the focus entirely on the Card Title and Action CTA.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure high contrast (Stone 900 text on Stone 100 background). Use bold, uppercase labels for tool tags to resemble shop-tag details.
- **Thumb Zone:** Place primary actions (like "Calculate Water Loss") towards the bottom of cards so they are easily reachable when holding a phone with one hand in the field.
