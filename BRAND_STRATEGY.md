# Brand and Conversion Copy Strategy: Solve

## 1. Positioning Statement
Solve provides simple, offline-ready calculators and rugged, snap-on monitors that help farmers and ranchers find utility waste, catch equipment trouble early, and fix operational failures fast.

## 2. Homepage Hero Options
- **Option 1:** Stop guessing. Find the leak. Fix it fast.
- **Option 2:** Find water waste and pump trouble before they cost you.
- **Option 3:** Practical diagnostics for folks who fix things themselves.

## 3. Homepage Subhead
Simple, rugged tools and snap-on monitors to track water leaks, fence faults, and freeze risks without the confusing manuals or IT guys. 

## 4. CTA Lines
- Calculate Your Waste
- Find Your Leak
- Plan Your Freeze Defense
- Log Pump Trouble
- Get the Right Monitor

## 5. Why This Exists
Farms and ranches bleed money through silent leaks, overworked pumps, and unseen fence faults. Most "smart farm" tech requires a degree in IT just to turn it on, trapping operators in confusing manuals and endless subscriptions. We built Solve because rural operators don't need digital transformation—they need a simple, reliable way to find the leak, track the problem, and prove the work got done. No tech guys required.

## 6. Style Guide: Words to Use vs. Words to Avoid

**Use These Words:**
- Fix, find, stop, catch, track, prove
- Leak, waste, fault, trouble, risk
- Rugged, practical, simple, snap-on, offline-ready
- Operator, owner, rancher, farmer, hand

**Avoid These Words:**
- Optimize, synergize, transform, disrupt
- Platform, ecosystem, cloud-native, AI-driven
- Innovative, cutting-edge, next-gen
- SaaS, digital transformation, enterprise, dashboard
