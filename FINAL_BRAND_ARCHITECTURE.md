# Final Brand Architecture & Copy System: Solve

## 1. Positioning Statement
Solve provides simple, offline-ready calculators and rugged, snap-on monitors that help rural operators find utility waste, catch equipment trouble early, and fix operational failures fast.

## 2. Final Hero Copy
- **Eyebrow:** RURAL DIAGNOSTICS & FIELD TOOLS
- **Headline:** Stop guessing. Find the leak. Fix it fast.
- **Subheadline:** Simple, rugged tools and snap-on monitors to track water leaks, fence faults, and freeze risks without confusing manuals or IT guys.
- **Primary CTA:** Pick Your Problem
- **Secondary CTA:** See Hardware Kits

## 3. Final Top-Nav Labels & Rationale
- **Fix:** Start directly from the operational problem (e.g., pump, fence, water) to find the right diagnostic path.
- **Calculators:** Run the numbers on waste, cost, risk, or ROI before spending money on a solution.
- **Kits:** Browse packaged hardware, software, and hybrid solutions ready to snap on and deploy in the field.
- **Favorites:** (Optional/Secondary) Quick access to the specific calculators, logs, and manuals you rely on most.

## 4. Homepage Section Order
1. **Hero** (Core promise & immediate routing)
2. **Problem Picker** (Navigate by what is broken: Water, Pumps, Freeze, Fence)
3. **Diagnostics & Calculators** (The Math: Estimate the damage)
4. **Hardware Kits** (The Fix: Recommended snap-on monitors)
5. **Trust & Proof** (Why we built this / who it's for)
6. **Footer** (Legal, contact, and network links)

## 5. Final Section Titles & Intros
- **Problem Picker:** 
  - *Title:* What's broken today?
  - *Intro:* Skip the guesswork and select a category to find the exact diagnostic tool for the job.
- **Diagnostics / Calculators:** 
  - *Title:* Estimate the Damage. Plan the Fix.
  - *Intro:* Use our field calculators to see exactly how much a problem is costing you and what it takes to solve it.
- **Hardware Kits:** 
  - *Title:* Rugged, Snap-On Monitors.
  - *Intro:* You found the leak—now fix it for good with field-tested hardware that works offline and requires no engineering degree.
- **Trust & Proof:** 
  - *Title:* Built for folks who fix things themselves.
  - *Intro:* We built Solve because rural operators need a simple, reliable way to find the leak, track the problem, and prove the work got done.

## 6. Tone Guide
**Words to Use:**
- Fix, find, stop, catch, track, prove
- Leak, waste, fault, trouble, risk, breakdown
- Rugged, practical, simple, snap-on, offline-ready, tactile
- Operator, owner, rancher, farmer, hand

**Words to Avoid:**
- Optimize, synergize, transform, disrupt, leverage
- Platform, ecosystem, cloud-native, AI-driven, synergistic
- Innovative, cutting-edge, next-gen, futuristic
- SaaS, digital transformation, enterprise, dashboard, holistic

## 7. Brand North Star
Solve is a digital workbench for rural operators, designed with the rugged reliability of a laminated field guide. We don’t sell "digital transformation" or smart-farm buzzwords; we provide practical tools to measure a problem in dollars and gallons, and the dead-simple hardware kits to fix it. Every page, button, and calculator must feel tactile, honest, and immediately useful to a person standing in the mud with a wrench.
